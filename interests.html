Interests & Hobbies

Exploring trails and roads on two wheels is one of my favorite ways to de-stress and connect with nature. Whether it's a challenging climb or a leisurely ride, cycling offers both physical and mental refreshment.

I enjoy diving into immersive video games, particularly strategy and RPG genres on PlayStation. It's a great way to appreciate interactive storytelling, complex systems, and creative problem-solving from a different perspective.

Continuous Learning

Beyond formal work, I'm always exploring new topics through books, tech blogs, and podcasts. Currently, I'm particularly interested in advancements in AI ethics and sustainable technology practices.

Hiking & Exploration

Getting outdoors and exploring local trails or parks helps me clear my head and appreciate the environment. Discovering new paths and viewpoints is always rewarding.
